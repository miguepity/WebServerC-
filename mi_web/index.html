Story of the Dark Side

The dark side of the Force, also known as the Bogan, or simply as Bad force,was an aspect of the Force.Individuals who used the dark side drew their power from more intense, raw and darker emotions such as fear, anger, hatred,passion, and aggression.The Sith were major practitioners of the dark side, and were the mortal enemies of the Jedi Order, who followed the light side of the Force. There were others such as the Inquisitorius, who, though not Sith, could draw upon the power of the dark side.
